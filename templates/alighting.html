Lighting Automation



Light is our passion good quality lighting design and installation can be an important factor in how comfortable your home is. Smart Homes are experts in residential lighting design, having worked on all types of homes and delivered amazing schemes around the world.

Lighting is a key element in any home, and it can be used to create a variety of different moods and atmospheres. Lighting can be used to create a warm and welcoming environment, or it can be used to create a more dramatic and intimate atmosphere. Lighting can also be used to create a more functional environment, such as in the kitchen or bathroom.

By integrating non-standard technologies with bespoke designs, we engineer unique solutions that transform the ways in which interiors are used and experienced. Our team has experience in supporting designers and architects to realise their creative visions, providing high-quality lighting solutions that are optimised to perform according to their individual needs. We have the skills, insights and resources needed to take on highly-complex projects and are well-versed in working to uphold commercial safety requirements, sustainability standards and budget constraints.

► Examples: Smart home lighting systems: These systems allow individuals to control the lighting in their homes from a central location, or remotely using a device such as a smartphone or tablet.

► Commercial lighting control systems: These systems allow building managers to control the lighting in a commercial building, reducing energy consumption and providing convenience.

► Outdoor lighting control systems: These systems allow individuals to control the lighting on their property, including landscaping lights, security lights, and holiday lights.

► Lighting automation systems can greatly improve energy efficiency and provide convenience, while allowing individuals to easily control the lighting in their building, room, or area.
