Access Control


Design, comfort, and security combined in one fingerprint access system. With the right interface, your fingers activate individual actions through the building control system. Access control systems can be based on physical devices (e.g. keycards, biometric systems) or logical systems (e.g. software-based authentication, passwords). The goal of an access control system is to enhance security and prevent unauthorized access.
