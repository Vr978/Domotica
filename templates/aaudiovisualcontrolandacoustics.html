Visual Control and Acoustics


Whether you are having a movie night in your theatre room with your family or are out for dinner at your favourite lounge, the balance of sound and elimination of noise is instrumental in elevating your entertainment experience.

Smart Homes specialise in equipping your space with audio and acoustics system to give you the most scintillating entertainment experience. Our automation architects advise the clients on the choice, alignment and placement of speakers for optimum audio.

Acoustic panels, tiles, ceiling material and sound dampening surface design are also a major part of our Acoustics portfolio.

► Audio equipment: microphones, speakers, amplifiers, headphones, sound cards, etc.

► Visual equipment: projectors, displays, cameras, lighting, etc.

► A/V production hardware: mixers, switchers, converters, etc.

► A/V presentation hardware: teleconferencing systems, interactive whiteboards, etc.

► A/V software: editing and post-production software, multimedia players, etc.
