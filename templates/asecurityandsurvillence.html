Security and Surveillance


Security is a feeling,It is the experience of feeling safe from any unwanted incidences and unforeseen situations. Security and surveillance solutions are a necessity to keep you and your loved ones away from any unpleasant incidences. At Smart Homes, we specialise in equipping you with security and surveillance technology that always keeps you one step ahead. We provide you with the best security and surveillance solutions that are designed to keep you safe and secure.

We provide you our Top-Notch services of our wide categories of security and survillence systmes.

► Burglar alarms: systems that detect unauthorized entry into a building or area and alert a monitoring center or the police.

► Fire alarms: systems that detect smoke, heat, or flames and alert authorities or building occupants.

► Video surveillance systems: cameras and recording equipment used to monitor and record activity in a specific area.

► Access control systems: technology that regulates and restricts entry into a building or area.

► Intrusion detection systems: systems that use sensors and alarms to detect and alert authorities to unauthorized entry into a protected area.

These security systems may be standalone or integrated into a larger security network and can be monitored and controlled remotely or locally.

► Closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems: cameras that are connected to a recording device or network, allowing live or recorded footage to be viewed and monitored.

► Network video recording (NVR) systems: digital systems that use IP cameras and software to record, store, and manage video footage.

► Wireless surveillance systems: systems that use wireless communication to transmit video and audio data from cameras to a remote monitoring device or location.
